Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation

The aim of Goal 6 is to ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all. Clean water is essential for health, reducing poverty, and ensuring sustainable development. Millions of people worldwide still lack access to safe drinking water and proper sanitation facilities.

Achieving this goal will improve health, protect the environment, and support economic growth.
