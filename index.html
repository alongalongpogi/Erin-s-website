SDG 6 — Clean Water & Sanitation

Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.

Why it matters | Targets | What you can do

Why Clean Water Matters

Access to safe water and sanitation prevents disease, improves education and helps communities thrive.

Main Targets (short list)

  • Safe and affordable drinking water for all.
  • Access to adequate sanitation and hygiene.
  • Improve water quality and reduce pollution.

Simple Actions You Can Take

Action Why it helps
Fix leaking taps Conserves water and lowers waste.
Use water-saving techniques Reduces demand and protects supply.
