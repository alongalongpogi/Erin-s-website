About SDG 6

SDG 6 aims to ensure access to safe water sources and sanitation for all people by 2030. Clean water is essential for health, reducing poverty, and ensuring sustainable development.

Why Clean Water Matters

Billions of people still lack safe drinking water and proper sanitation. Access to clean water prevents diseases, supports education, and helps communities grow stronger.

What You Can Do

  • Save water by turning off taps when not in use.
  • Fix leaks at home to avoid water waste.
  • Join community clean-up drives to protect water sources.
  • Educate others about the importance of clean water.
