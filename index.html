Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation

Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all people. This goal aims to provide access to safe and affordable drinking water, sanitation, and hygiene for everyone.

Why Is It Important?

Clean water is essential for life, health, and the environment. Without access to safe water and sanitation, diseases spread quickly, and communities struggle to survive. Achieving this goal helps improve education, reduce poverty, and create a healthier planet.

What Can We Do?

  • Turn off the tap while brushing your teeth.
  • Report water leaks and fix dripping faucets.
  • Use a pail instead of a hose when cleaning.
  • Join clean water and sanitation projects.
  • Educate others about saving water.
