United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 6

Clean Water and Sanitation

Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation

Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.

Why is it Important?

Billions of people worldwide still lack safe drinking water and proper sanitation. Achieving this goal improves health, reduces poverty, and protects the environment.

What Can We Do?

- Save water by fixing leaks
- Use water responsibly
- Support clean water projects
- Educate others about sanitation
